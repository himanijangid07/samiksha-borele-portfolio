Dr. Samiksha Borele

President, Borele World

Dr. Samiksha Borele is a visionary leader and President of Borele World, a renowned real estate and hospitality company. With a decade-long tenure, she has been instrumental in shaping the company's future, driving growth, and exploring new ventures.

Her expertise spans real estate, marketing, and international business, backed by prestigious degrees from IIPM (MBA in Marketing and HR) and La Trobe University (MBA in International Business Management).

Under her leadership, Borele World has expanded its footprint, diversified its offerings, and established itself as a trusted name in the industry. Dr. Borele's strategic vision and dedication to excellence have earned her respect across the business community.
