About

Dr. Samiksha Borele is a Visionary Leader, a Seasoned Entrepreneur, Positive Mindset Mentor and President of Borele World, a renowned Real Estate and Hospitality company. She is also the Trustee – Borele Foundation, Director at Bhaiji Infraprojests Pvt. Ltd., SB Hospitality Pvt. Ltd., and Ruchika Infra Pvt. Ltd. Dr. Samiksha Borele is a dynamic and visionary leader with extensive expertise in real estate development, hospitality management, marketing strategy, human resources, international business, and leadership. With a track record of success in business growth, strategic planning, and operational excellence, she has been instrumental in shaping the trajectory of Borele World, a premier enterprise known for its excellence in real estate, hospitality, and business innovation. Dr. Samiksha Borele is a renowned Life Coach, Positive Mindset Mentor, Leadership Mentor, and Wellness Mentor, dedicated to transforming lives through the power of mindset and perspective. Dr. Samiksha Borele is a firm advocate for the transformative power of a positive mindset. She wholeheartedly believes that cultivating a resilient and optimistic outlook is the cornerstone of achieving success in all aspects of life, including entrepreneurship, business, and startups.
